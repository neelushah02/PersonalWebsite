Hi, I'm Neel.

I'm currently a second-year Davis Scholar at Northwestern University, where I major in Economics with minors in History and Computer Science.
I'm interested in data ethics, fiscal policy and global climate governance. I also enjoy studying nationalisms, clean energy and histories of decolonization.

At Northwestern, I've worked at the Institute for Sustainability and Energy developing syllabi for graduate-level courses. I've also developed business strategy for the non-profit Give Essential, and have worked on NLP-based data analytics at Basil Labs.

On the weekends you can find me drinking tea, talking soccer and tending to my garden—often all at the same time :)
