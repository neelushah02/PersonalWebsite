Hi, I'm Neel.

I recently graduated with honors from Northwestern University, where I was a Davis Scholar majoring in Economics and Computer Science, with a minor in History.

I'm passionate about fiscal policy, artificial intelligence and decarbonization. I also enjoy translating poetry and reading about the Soviet Union.

Most recently, I was a strategy consulting intern at EY-Parthenon's Chicago office, where I'll be returning full-time as an Associate. While at Northwestern, I worked for the Institute for Sustainability and Energy developing syllabi, and was a Leopold Fellow researching comparative tax policy.

On the weekends you can find me drinking tea, playing/talking soccer and reading—often all at the same time!
